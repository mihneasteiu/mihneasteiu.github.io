About me

Hello! I'm Mihnea-Gabriel Steiu, a student at Brown University, pursuing Applied Mathematics & Computer Science with a 4.0 GPA. My passion lies in leveraging technology in the benefit of society and reducing risks arising from advanced artificial intelligence systems. I firmly believe that AI holds immense power to influence humanity both in beneficial and catastrophic ways, and it is our duty to prioritize our safety. So far I have gotten involved in diverse research experiences, from developing visualization apps for blind people to building deep learning algorithms for AI-based cancer treatment methods. As the founder of a globally competitive robotics team, I've combined leadership with technical expertise in computer science and AI. Having experience in both frontend and backend programming, I'm always eager to take on new challenges that blend mathematics, computer science, and innovative problem-solving. When not coding, you might find me performing music, playing basketball, or reading.
